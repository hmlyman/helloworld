My Future Plans

I don't plan too far in advance. We used to move to different states every 6 months or so for my husband's job, so planning in advance was pointless. We now live in Utah for the forseeable future which is quite nice. Since I still hesitate to plan too far in advance, I have a few ideas of what I would like to accomplish in the future. Most of those ideas relate to this class and future career paths stemming from succeeding in this course.
My future plans are as follows:

  • Successfully complete this program
  • Find a career in the tech industry
  • Purchase a home with my family
  • Raise healthy, happy kids
  • Maybe vacation sometimes
  • Maybe vacation with kids sometimes
  • Continue to be a taxi for my children

If I can successfully complete all of these plans, I plan
to dance happily.
