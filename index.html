This is Heather Lyman's Live Webpage

Welcome to my webpage! In this webpage, you'll find out everything
you never wanted to know about me! To start, I'm from Texas and
attended Texas A&M University where I received a Bachelor's degree.
I met my husband in college and we married just before I graduated.
We have three kids and have moved 16 different times to many different
states. I have definitely not loved every state we've lived in, but
the experiences and people we've met have made it all worth it.

My kids are all going to school this year and it has made me switch
gears to find what I want to do as a career for the rest of my life.
Because of the job security and interesting premise, I felt the tech
industry would be a great field to get into. I also think it's important
for my kids to understand and be interested in tech. Hopefully they will
be able to glean some of that from me.
I agree with Vincent Cerf when he said,
I just am a huge cheerleader for getting kids interested in
science and technology.

My main hurdle is to make my brain think more logically than it does.
Unfortunately, my brain is quite far from logical thinking.
I can definitely understand and appreciate logic, and I love it. However,
it is not my brain's default. That being said,
I'm excited to push my brain to new limits!
