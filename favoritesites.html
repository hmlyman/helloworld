My Favorite Sites

I really enjoy stories. I love to listen to people tell stories,
watch them play out on screens, and write them.
This brings me to my list of favorite sites. They are all based on
watching or listening to different stories. I know it may sound
like I'm an 80 year old woman "watching my stories" on TV,
but it really is the story aspect that pulls me in. It isn't important
to me to have optimal screen sizes or resolutions. I really just want to
know what happens. Therefore, my favorite sites to visit are as follows:

  1. Audible
  2. Hulu
  3. Netflix
  4. YouTube
  5. GoodReads

I am often listening to books from audible while hiking or running or
cleaning my house. It keeps my brain busy and thinking outside of its
comfort zone. I also really like TV. I grew up watching a ton of TV,
because screen time wasn't a thing yet. It's how I relax... unless
it's a scary show. Then it's how I stay up all night imagining the worst
possible scenarios that could happen while I sleep. Super fun.
